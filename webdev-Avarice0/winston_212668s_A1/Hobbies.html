My hobbies!

Gaming

I like playing minecraft in my free time. Minecraft is an open world sandbox that explores creativity and survival into a procedurally generated world. Its a casual simulator game with many options of playing. Such a building and exploring creativity. Or hardcore survival aspects that can get really intense!

Strategy

I also like playing strategy and 4X games and colony simulators. Using my brain to think of future possibilities and counters to them, making me stand at the top whenever possible. Not only that, I enjoy controlling a colony to do my bidding, shaping the world into a utopia for my citizens.

Cooking

I like cooking in my free time. I'm not a chef nor a professional by any standards, but I strive to improve and hope to be confident in my skills. I want to be able to cook for my friends when they come over and extend my hospility beyond food as well.!

Music

I really enjoy music and have dabbled in piano and composing. I have gravitated towards EDM, Rock and Pop genres. However, I am not disposed to just these! I wish to learn more about music and create a style that truly defines me, then compose pieces to be used in my games. Anything from country, atmospheric, retro to even alternative, I want to learn about them all!
