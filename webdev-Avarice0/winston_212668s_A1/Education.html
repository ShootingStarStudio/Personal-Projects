My Past Experiences

Admiralty Primary School

Admiralty Primary School was the primary school I attended for 6 years. Filled with playful classmates and patient teachers, I learnt alot about the world, math, science, social studies and languages. Despite it being a small neighbourhood school, I am proud to have come this far and owe alot of my foundations to Admiralty Primary School.

ZhongHua Secondary School

ZhongHua Secondary School was where I had the most memories of. Practical sessions teaching the fundamentals of a subject, lecture based curriculum were all part of my education there. It allowed me to be accustomed to tertiary education at NYP. It was also where I first garnered an interest for coding! Python was an examinable subject and I participated in the third batch of O'Level candidates. My computing lecturer was an experienced coder that taught us everything we needed to know about python, well... not everything but it was enough for me to get amazing grades!

Game Development @ NYP

Where am I now? Right, I am at Nanyang Polytechnic's School of Design & Media. Here, I am furthering myself to be a game developer. I may not be the best, but I put in 100% of effort and strive to be at my best performance everyday. It may be hard at times, confusing, or even outright demanding, but I refuse to give up until I can make a game that I'm proud of.
